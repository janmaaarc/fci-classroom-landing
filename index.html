Core Learning Modules

Comprehensive courses designed to take you from beginner to confident furniture expert.

01

Principles of Modern Design

Explore the evolution of "Form Follows Function." Study the hallmarks of Mid-Century Modern, Minimalism, and Industrial styles.

02

The Art of Selection

Master spatial awareness, scale, and proportion to ensure pieces fit harmoniously within a room's architecture.

Understand the characteristics of wood, metal, leather, and textiles. Learn to evaluate construction quality.

04

Curating Your Space

Develop your eye for creating cohesive interiors. Learn the principles of layering and focal points.
